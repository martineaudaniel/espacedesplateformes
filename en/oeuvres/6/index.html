Joyce Cimper

The soundscapes are audio arrangements that blend various sounds to recreate the atmosphere of iconic locations from a television series, such as the main characters' rooms or the café where they frequently gather. Dialogue excerpts, common in these audio creations, enhance the feeling of being with the protagonists, close enough to hear their conversations and take part in them. ASMR roleplay also allows for a special time spent with one’s favorite characters. ASMRtists portray fictional heroes in these creations, maintaining eye contact with viewers by looking directly at the camera lens. They speak directly to the audience, asking questions and reacting to their answers, always in a soft voice since the aim of ASMR is to induce a state of relaxation. Ultimately, ASMR roleplay aims not only to relax the audience but also to offer scenarios that extend the storylines developed within the series. Shifting videos, on the other hand, are designed to help individuals transition from the real world to the fictional universe of their choice. Precise instructions may be provided to the audience. Most videos, however, pair series music with subliminal images, allowing viewers to visualize the characters they will interact with and the spaces where they can live the adventures they desire once they reach the reality they envision.
